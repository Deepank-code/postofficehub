1b:Tdc1,<h2>Post Office Kisan Vikas Patra (KVP)</h2>
<p>The <strong>Kisan Vikas Patra (KVP)</strong> is a <strong>government-backed savings scheme</strong> ideal for individuals seeking <strong>guaranteed, risk-free growth of their investments</strong>, making it a secure option for stable financial planning.</p>
<hr>
<h3>Key Highlights</h3>
<p>‚úÖ <strong>7.5% per annum compounded annually</strong><br>
‚úÖ Investment <strong>doubles in 115 months (9 years and 7 months)</strong><br>
‚úÖ Minimum investment: ‚Çπ1000; No maximum limit<br>
‚úÖ Safe, government-guaranteed returns<br>
‚úÖ Flexible and easy to operate</p>
<hr>
<h3>Latest Interest Rate (From 01 Jan 2024)</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>7.5% compounded annually</strong></li>
<li>Investment <strong>doubles in 115 months</strong></li>
</ul>
<hr>
<h3>Salient Features</h3>
<h4>(a) Who Can Open:</h4>
<ul>
<li>A single adult</li>
<li>Joint account (up to 3 adults)</li>
<li>Guardian on behalf of a minor or a person of unsound mind</li>
<li>A minor above 10 years in their own name</li>
</ul>
<hr>
<h4>(b) Deposit:</h4>
<ul>
<li>Minimum deposit: ‚Çπ1000</li>
<li>Subsequent deposits in multiples of ‚Çπ100</li>
<li>No maximum limit on investment</li>
<li>Any number of accounts can be opened</li>
</ul>
<hr>
<h4>(c) Maturity:</h4>
<ul>
<li>The deposit matures as per the <strong>Ministry of Finance guidelines applicable on the date of deposit</strong></li>
<li>At the current rate, <strong>the invested amount doubles in 115 months</strong></li>
</ul>
<hr>
<h4>(d) Pledging as Security:</h4>
<p>KVP can be pledged or transferred as security by submitting a prescribed application form with an acceptance letter from the pledgee. Pledging is allowed to:</p>
<ul>
<li>The President of India or State Governor</li>
<li>RBI, Scheduled Banks, Co-operative Banks/Societies</li>
<li>Corporations (public/private), Government Companies, Local Authorities</li>
<li>Housing Finance Companies</li>
</ul>
<hr>
<h4>(e) Premature Closure:</h4>
<p>Premature closure of KVP is allowed under the following conditions:</p>
<ul>
<li>On the death of the sole account holder, or any/all account holders in case of a joint account</li>
<li>On forfeiture by a pledgee (e.g., Gazetted Officer)</li>
<li>On order by the court</li>
<li>After <strong>2 years and 6 months</strong> from the date of deposit</li>
</ul>
<hr>
<h4>(f) Transfer of Account:</h4>
<p>KVP can be transferred from one person to another under specific circumstances:</p>
<ul>
<li>On the death of the account holder, to the nominee or legal heirs</li>
<li>From the deceased account holder to surviving joint holder(s)</li>
<li>On court order</li>
<li>On pledging the account to a specified authority</li>
</ul>
<hr>
<h3>Why Choose Post Office KVP?</h3>
<p>‚úÖ <strong>Guaranteed doubling of investment without market risks</strong><br>
‚úÖ <strong>Ideal for safe, long-term wealth building</strong><br>
‚úÖ <strong>No maximum limit, making it flexible for larger investments</strong><br>
‚úÖ <strong>Can be pledged as security for loans</strong><br>
‚úÖ <strong>Available at all post offices across India</strong></p>
