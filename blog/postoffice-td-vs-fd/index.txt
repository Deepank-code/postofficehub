<h2>What is a Post Office Time Deposit?</h2>
<ul>
<li>Offered by <strong>India Post</strong> under the <strong>Small Savings Schemes</strong>.</li>
<li>Tenure options: 1, 2, 3, and 5 years.</li>

<li>Minimum investment: ‚Çπ1000, no maximum limit.</li>

<h2>What is a Fixed Deposit (FD)?</h2>
<ul>
<li>Offered by <strong>banks and NBFCs</strong>.</li>

<li>Interest can be paid monthly, quarterly, or at maturity.</li>
