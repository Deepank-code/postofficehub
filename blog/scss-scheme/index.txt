1b:Tfcb,<h2>Post Office Senior Citizen Savings Scheme (SCSS)</h2>
<p>The <strong>Post Office Senior Citizen Savings Scheme (SCSS)</strong> offers <strong>safe, high-interest earnings for senior citizens</strong>, providing <strong>financial security post-retirement with quarterly interest payouts</strong> and tax-saving benefits under Section 80C.</p>
<hr>
<h3>Key Highlights</h3>
<p>✅ <strong>8.2% per annum interest, paid quarterly</strong><br>
✅ Investment in multiples of ₹1000 up to ₹30 lakh<br>
✅ Safe, government-backed investment<br>
✅ Tax benefits under Section 80C<br>
✅ Ideal for retirees seeking stable income</p>
<hr>
<h3>Latest Interest Rate (From 01 Jan 2024)</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>8.2% per annum, payable quarterly.</strong></li>
</ul>
<p>Interest is payable from the date of deposit to the next quarter-end (31 March, 30 June, 30 September, 31 December) and on the first day of each subsequent quarter.</p>
<hr>
<h3>Salient Features</h3>
<h4>(a) Who Can Open:</h4>
<ul>
<li>Individuals aged <strong>60 years and above</strong></li>
<li>Retired Civilian Employees (55–60 years), within 1 month of receiving retirement benefits</li>
<li>Retired Defense Employees (50–60 years), within 1 month of receiving retirement benefits</li>
<li>Accounts can be opened individually or jointly with a spouse (full deposit attributed to the first holder)</li>
</ul>
<hr>
<h4>(b) Deposit:</h4>
<ul>
<li>Minimum: ₹1000; in multiples of ₹1000</li>
<li>Maximum: ₹30 lakh in all SCSS accounts held by an individual</li>
<li>Excess deposits will be refunded, and PO Savings Account interest will apply from the date of excess deposit to the date of refund</li>
<li>Eligible for <strong>Section 80C tax benefits</strong></li>
</ul>
<hr>
<h4>(c) Interest:</h4>
<ul>
<li>Payable quarterly: 1 April, 1 July, 1 October, 1 January</li>
<li>Unclaimed interest does not earn additional interest</li>
<li>Can be credited automatically to a Post Office Savings Account or via ECS</li>
<li>Interest is taxable:
<ul>
<li>TDS applies if total interest across SCSS accounts exceeds ₹50,000 in a financial year</li>
<li>Submit Form 15G/15H to avoid TDS if within the limit</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<hr>
<h4>(d) Premature Closure:</h4>
<ul>
<li>Allowed anytime after opening</li>
<li>If closed before 1 year:
<ul>
<li>No interest payable; paid interest recovered from principal</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>If closed after 1 year but before 2 years:
<ul>
<li>1.5% of principal deducted</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>If closed after 2 years but before 5 years:
<ul>
<li>1% of principal deducted</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Extended accounts can be closed after 1 year from extension without deductions</li>
</ul>
<hr>
<h4>(e) Maturity and Closure:</h4>
<ul>
<li>The account matures after <strong>5 years</strong> from the date of opening</li>
<li>On the depositor’s death:
<ul>
<li>The account earns PO Savings Account interest from the date of death</li>
<li>If the spouse is a joint holder/nominee and eligible, the account can continue until maturity without interruption</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<hr>
<h4>(f) Extension of Account:</h4>
<ul>
<li>Extendable for <strong>3 years</strong> after maturity</li>
<li>Extension request to be submitted within 1 year from maturity</li>
<li>Extended accounts earn interest at the rate applicable on the maturity date</li>
</ul>
<hr>
<h3>Why Choose Post Office SCSS?</h3>
<p>✅ <strong>Secure high-interest returns for retirees</strong><br>
✅ <strong>Quarterly payouts for regular income</strong><br>
✅ <strong>Capital safety with government backing</strong><br>
✅ <strong>Tax-saving benefits under Section 80C</strong><br>
✅ <strong>Simple management at your nearest Post Office</strong></p>
<hr>
<p>The <strong>Post Office Senior Citizen Savings Scheme</strong> is a reliable option for senior citizens and retirees to <strong>secure steady, high-interest income while preserving their capital safely</strong>. Start your investment in SCSS today to ensure a stress-free retirement with financial stability.</p>
